Josh's Brownies

These are a slightly altered recipe of something I origonally found online, they contain a bucket load of sugar and added espresso powder. Delicious.

Recipe

  • CHOCOLATE
  • BOWL All crushed dark chocolate, Q.cup cocoa powder, 1 table spoon espresso powder
  • HEAT 1.25 blocks butter until it bubbles.
  • POUR the butter over the chocolate.
  • WET MIX
  • BOWL sugar, brown sugar, vanilla extract, salt
  • CRACK 6 eggs into a seperate bowl.
  • ADD 1 egg at a time into the ingredients.
  • BEAT the mixture to incorporate lots of air.
  • POUR The chocolate mixture in slowly while mixing.
  • DRY INGREDIENTS
  • SIFT flour into the wet mix
  • SIFT Hcup Cocoa Powder
  • FOLD the mixture in until gooey
  • BAKE
  • 180c in the oven for 20 minutes.
  • SLAM brownies on counter (crack mixture).
  • SPRINKLE salt over the half cooked mix.
  • 180c in the oven for 25 more minutes.
  • INGREDIENTS
  • 230g Dark chocolate.
  • Q.Cup Cocoa Powder
  • 1TbSp Espresso Powder
  • 312g Unsalted Butter (1.25 Blocks)
  • 2Cup White Sugar
  • HCup Brown Sugar
  • 2TbSp Vanilla Extract
  • 2TbSp Salt
  • 6 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Flour
